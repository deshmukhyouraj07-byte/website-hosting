Welcome to the 2025 Global Health Portal. The WHO is the specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health. In 2025, WHO leads the world in managing health emergencies and ensuring universal health coverage.

WHO works worldwide to promote health, keep the world safe, and serve the vulnerable. By 2025, the organization is focused on the 14th General program of works (GPW 14), aiming to provide 5 billion people with better health and well-being.

2025 Global Campaigns

WORLD HEALTH DAY 2025

The 2025 theme is "Healthy Beginnings, Hopeful Futures." It focuses on ending preventable maternal and newborn deaths

HEALTH STRATEGY 2028

The WHO 2025-2028 roadmap prioritizes climate-resilient health systems and pandemic preparedness for all nations.

Major Health Pillars in 2025

    universal heath coverage - A sheild

    The 2025 mission is to ensure everyone, everywhere can access essential health services without falling into poverty. It provides the structural rigidity of a national health system.

     Health Emergency Preparedness – The Skin

    Like skin, this pillar protects the world from external threats. WHO coordinates responses to outbreaks and climate-related disasters to keep global populations safe.

     Essential Medicines – The Production Line

    The WHO produces guidelines and lists of essential medicines and vaccines. These are the building blocks required for doctors to treat diseases and speed up recovery worldwide.

     The Secretariat – The Brain

    Located in Geneva, this acts as the control centre. It manages the global health agenda and coordinates with all member states to ensure policy alignment and rapid response.

     data Analytics – The Life Fluid

    Health data is the cytoplasm of the organization. It fills the gaps in knowledge and is responsible for regulating how resources are transported to areas in most need.
