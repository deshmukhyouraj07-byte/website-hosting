World Health Day 2025

"Healthy Beginnings, Hopeful Futures"

Campaign Objective: In 2025, the World Health Organization has launched its most ambitious maternal health initiative yet. The goal is to reduce global maternal mortality by 30% over the next three years.

Key 2025 Action Points:

  • Universal Prenatal Access: Ensuring every pregnant woman has at least 8 clinical contacts during pregnancy.
  • Newborn Nutrition: Promoting exclusive breastfeeding for the first 6 months to ensure "Healthy Beginnings."
  • Digital Health Kits: Deploying mobile health monitoring for mothers in remote rural areas.

Join the global movement. Use the hashtag #HealthyBeginnings2025 to share your story and support health equity for every mother and child.
