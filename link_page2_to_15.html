2025 - 2028 STRATEGIC ROADMAP

STRATEGY 1: CLIMATE RESILIENCE

Protecting health systems from extreme weather and ensuring hospitals remain operational during climate disasters in 2025.

STRATEGY 2: PANDEMIC ACCORD

A global agreement to share vaccines and medical data faster to stop outbreaks before they become global pandemics.

STRATEGY 3: DIGITAL INNOVATION

Using 2025 technology and AI to monitor disease trends and provide remote healthcare to people in isolated areas.

STRATEGY 4: HEALTH WORKFORCE

Training and supporting millions of new doctors and nurses to reach the goal of Universal Health Coverage by 2028.

For more details, visit the page www.who.int.
